Computer Science and Engineering Association

About Us

The Computer Science and Engineering Association (CSEA) is an integral part of the Computer Science and Engineering Department. It recognizes the innovative leaders in a variety of fields of application, advanced study and research in the field of CSE. The members of the association will be known for their passion for the development of CSE students in the frontiers of knowledge, expertise and innovation. We enable the students to have a broad foundation and awareness of core concepts in computer science by allowing them to form significant original research and report that in a scholarly fashion. The association will continue to develop and administer programs which serve the needs of industrial computer scientists, researchers, and computer application specialists for whom the discipline of computing sciences provides indispensable tools.


Vision & Mission:

To see every computer science engineer as a pragmatic programmer with strong interpersonal skills and achieving this excellence together.


  • 1. To promote knowledge sharing, enhancement of computing, coding and communication skills among the students through frequent technical talks, workshops, courses and programming contests.

  • 2. To create an ambience that nurtures the holistic development of the students, encouraging and empowering curiosity, brilliance, finesse and professionalism within them.

  • 3. To direct students towards fostering their innovation and problemsolving skills, leadership qualities, team-spirit and ethical responsibilities.

  • 4. To spur heuristic learning approach which the industry needs today.

  • 5. To organize incessant technical and non-technical activities and rack up the goals.

  • 6. To provide an introductory platform to emerging technologies.
